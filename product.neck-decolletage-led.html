Have questions?

What color wavelengths does the Neck & Décolletage LED System have?

The Neck & Décolletage LED Device uses two clinically backed wavelengths of light to target visible signs of aging, sun damage, and skin elasticity on the neck and chest:

  • Red Light (633nm): Penetrates the upper layers of the skin to boost collagen and elastin, improving firmness, reducing fine lines, and smoothing texture.
  • Near-Infrared Light (830nm): Reaches deeper into the skin to support tissue repair, cell regeneration, and circulation, helping reduce inflammation and restore elasticity.

Together, these wavelengths stimulate your skin’s natural healing processes to reveal smoother, firmer, more even-toned skin over time. All without heat, pain, or downtime.

How quickly will I see results?

Every individual may respond differently to specific wavelengths of lights and results will depend on the goal you want to achieve.

Visible improvements can typically be seen within 4 weeks of consistent use. More significant changes can be seen over several months.

The day-to-day changes will be subtle, so be sure to take "before and after" pictures to document progress. The most important thing to remember is that results are cumulative, so consistent daily treatment is key.

Is it safe for all skin types?

Yes, this device is safe for all skin types. It is non-invasive and gentle on the skin. However, if you have specific skin conditions, we recommend consulting with a dermatologist before use.

How often can I use the treatment?

For best results, we recommend using the device daily.

How long is one treatment?

Just 10 minutes! A one and a half hour charge can deliver up to 7 to 8 treatments.

Can I use it on other areas of the body?

While it's primarily designed for the neck and chest, some users apply it to their face or other body areas such as the back and hands. Refer to the user manual for specific usage recommendations.

Can I use skincare products with the device?

For optimal results use the device on clean, bare skin. Creating a barrier between the device and your skin with unauthorized skincare products may diminish its effectiveness or potentially cause skin reactions.

Can you use this while pregnant or breastfeeding?

Pregnant and breastfeeding individuals should exercise caution when considering any new skincare or beauty treatment. It is generally recommended to consult with a healthcare professional, before using such devices during pregnancy or while breastfeeding.
