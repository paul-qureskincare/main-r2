Have questions?

What makes Dermal Mist different from other facial sprays?

Unlike many face mists that are fragranced or water-heavy with little skin benefit, our Dermal Mist is formulated specifically for sensitive and post-treatment skin.

Dermal Mist is powered by hypochlorous acid and enriched with chamomile, offering anti-inflammatory and antibacterial benefits without any harsh chemicals. It's pH balanced and gentle enough for all skin types, making it a versatile addition to your daily routine.

It hydrates without irritation, soothes redness, and helps your skin bounce back faster.

What are the benefits of Dermal Mist and hypochlorous acid?

Our Dermal Mist is powered by hypochlorous acid — a gentle yet effective ingredient known for its soothing, antibacterial, and barrier-supporting properties.

Hypochlorous acid (HOCl) helps soothe redness, reduce inflammation, and accelerate healing, making it especially useful for sensitive, acne-prone, or post-treatment skin.

Unlike harsher actives, HOCl supports the skin barrier while eliminating harmful bacteria — all without causing dryness or disrupting the skin’s natural balance. It is safe for all skin types, it’s a gentle but effective way to calm, protect, and refresh the skin.

How should I incorporate Dermal Mist into my skincare routine?

You can use Dermal Mist morning and evening as a purifying step before applying treatments. It’s also great for refreshing your skin throughout the day, post-workout, or to disinfect surfaces such as your pillow and your skincare tools.

What’s the concentration of hypochlorous acid in Dermal Mist?

Dermal Mist contains a precise 0.02% concentration of hypochlorous acid, which is effective enough to deliver antibacterial and anti-inflammatory benefits while remaining gentle on the skin.

We don’t add any fragrances, so you may notice the light, natural scent of hypochlorous acid. This should fade soon after spraying.

Can I use Dermal Mist on sensitive skin?

Absolutely. Dermal Mist is dermatologically tested, hypoallergenic, and formulated specifically to be kind to even the most sensitive skin. It’s designed to soothe, purify, and protect without causing irritation.

Is this product vegan and cruelty-free?

Yes, Dermal Mist is both vegan and cruelty-free. We are committed to creating products that are kind to both your skin and the environment.
