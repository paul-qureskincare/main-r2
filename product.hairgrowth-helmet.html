Have questions?

How does the Qure Q-Renew LLLT Helmet work?

The Q-Renew LLLT Helmet uses a combination of low-level laser therapy (LLLT) and red LED light therapy to help energize hair follicles and support healthier-looking, fuller hair. The lasers deliver focused light deeper into the scalp, while LEDs provide broader coverage

How often can I use this treatment?

We recommend using the helmet 3-4 times per week for at least 16 weeks. After that, continue every other day to maintain results.

Each treatment lasts 12 minutes and ends automatically.

How quickly will I see the results?

Visible changes take time. Most users should expect to use the helmet consistently for at least 3-6 months before noticing fuller, healthier-looking hair. Like any long-term treatment, consistency is key!

Can I use it while it is charging?

No. The helmet will not operate while charging. Always unplug before use.

How long does it take to charge?

A full charge takes about 4 hours. The helmet is powered by a rechargeable 3.7V, 5000mAh lithium-ion battery.

Is Q-Renew LLLT Helmet safe and FDA cleared?

Yes, the Q-Renew LLLT Helmet is safe and FDA-cleared.

The Q-Renew LLLT Helmet is indicated for the promotion of hair growth in males who have Norwood-Hamilton classifications of IIa-V, and in females with androgenetic alopecia who have Ludwig-Savin Classifications of I-II, and in both, Fitzpatrick Classification of Skin Phototypes I to IV.

It features built-in safety sensors that only activate the lights when the device is properly positioned on your head. It will automatically pause if removed. The warmth is mild, with a maximum of 35°C (95°F).

Who is it not recommended for?

Do not use this product if you are under 18, pregnant, breastfeeding, photosensitive, on light-sensitizing medications, prone to light-triggered headaches or seizures, or have open wounds or infections on the scalp.

Does Q-Renew LLLT Helmet come with a warranty?

Yes, the Q-Renew LLLT Helmet comes with a 2-year warranty.
