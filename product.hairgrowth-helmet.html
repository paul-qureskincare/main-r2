Yes, the Q-Renew LLLT Helmet is safe and FDA-cleared.

The Q-Renew LLLT Helmet is indicated for the promotion of hair growth in males who have Norwood-Hamilton classifications of IIa-V, and in females with androgenetic alopecia who have Ludwig-Savin Classifications of I-II, and in both, Fitzpatrick Classification of Skin Phototypes I to IV.

It features built-in safety sensors that only activate the lights when the device is properly positioned on your head. It will automatically pause if removed. The warmth is mild, with a maximum of 35°C (95°F).

Do not use this product if you are under 18, pregnant, breastfeeding, photosensitive, on light-sensitizing medications, prone to light-triggered headaches or seizures, or have open wounds or infections on the scalp.
