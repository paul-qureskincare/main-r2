How To Install

To install your new Shower Filter, you'll need the following:

  • Qure Shower Filter
  • (Optional) Plumber's tape for extra leak protection (provided)
  • (Optional) A towel or cloth for better grip when unscrewing the showerhead
Step-By-Step Guide
Step 1

Remove your old showerhead by unscrewing it from the wall or shower arm.

Step 2

Attach your new Qure filter by twisting the filter onto the shower pipe.

Make sure the rubber washer is inside to prevent leaks.

Step 3

Tighten until secure. Make sure it's snug but not forced.

Step 4

Let it run for 2-3 minutes to flush out any loose particles.

Done! You're ready to enjoy filtered, skin-friendly water.

Quick How To Replace The Filter Guide

We recommend replacing your filter every 3 months to ensure optimal filtration.

You may find that water flow slows sooner than this 3 month guideline. In this case, you can replace the filter sooner to maintain strong flow and effective filtration.

To replace the filter, you'll need a new replacement filter cartridge.

Step-By-Step Guide
Step 1

Turn off the shower. Rotate the shower cover counterclockwise to remove it.

Step 2

Remove the old cartridge and replace with a new cartridge. Firmly press the new cartridge into the filter housing.

Step 3

Replace the shower cover and turn clockwise to secure it.

Step 4

Turn on the shower and let the system flush for 2-3 minutes to wash out any initial impurities.

Care & Maintenance

To ensure your Qure Shower Filter works optimally, regular maintenance is important:

  • Wipe the filter's surface with a damp cloth to remove soap scum and build-up.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals when cleaning, and do not use steel wool, abrasive cleaners, or corrosive liquids.
  • Replace the filter every 3 months, or sooner if the water flow slows down.
  • Periodically check the connections to ensure there are no leaks.

Troubleshooting & Adjustments

Having trouble? Don't worry, most issues are quick to solve.

Water leaks

Check that the shower head is tightly secured, and plumber tape is correctly applied. Add more tape if needed.

Odor in the water

If you notice any unusual odors, flush the filter by running water through the system for several minutes. Remove and clean or replace the filter as needed.

Low or uneven water pressure

Low flow can occur for a couple of reasons if the filter is clogged. If the pressure from your Qure Shower Filter feels too low, try these steps:

  • Check the washer and flow restrictor: Unscrew the filter from your shower arm and look inside both ends. Some homes have low water pressure already, and the built-in restrictor might be making it worse. You can remove the small black rubber ring with a plastic insert (the flow restrictor) to improve pressure.
  • Clean the mesh screen: Sometimes debris from your water supply can clog the filter mesh. Remove and rinse the filter under water or gently scrub it with a toothbrush to remove any impurities.
  • Make sure it's fully tightened: A loose connection can cause pressure loss. Reinstall the filter, making sure it's snug but not overtightened.
