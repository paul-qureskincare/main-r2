Have questions?

How quickly will I see results?

Most people see improvement within the first weeks of using Qure products, but this can vary.

Some Qure products deliver visible results quickly, like smoother, more hydrated skin after your first Micro-Infusion session. For long-term improvements in wrinkles, acne, or hyperpigmentation, we recommend consistent use for at least 4 to 12 weeks. Like any effective routine, results build over time.

How often can I use Qure treatments?

It depends on the product.

Most of our products, including our All-In-One Serum and Q-Rejuvalight Pro LED Mask, are gentle and intended for daily use. Our Micro-Infusion treatments are more intensive and should be used less frequently. We recommend using the Micro=Infusion Targeted Eye Patches once per week. and the full Micro-Infusion System once every 2 weeks, for the best results.

Always follow the usage guidelines for each product and adjust based on how your skin responds.

Can I use multiple Qure products together?

Yes, absolutely. Qure products are designed to complement each other as part of a complete routine.
