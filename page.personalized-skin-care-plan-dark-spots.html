Skincare is a lifestyle

Caring for your skin goes beyond the products you use. It’s about adopting habits that support your skin’s health every day. Our dermatologists recommend these expert tips to help you reach your skin goals even faster.

  • Protect your skin with SPF

    Daily SPF is a must. It’s the easiest way to protect your skin from UV damage and prevent sun spots and dark spots from forming.

  • Reduce your sugar intake

    Studies show that excess sugar and processed food can lead to increased inflammation which may worsen pigmentation.

  • Nourish your skin with good fats

    Foods rich in omega-3s (like salmon and walnuts) support your skin’s elasticity and keep it looking firm and even.
