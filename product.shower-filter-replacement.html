Have questions?

How will the Qure Shower Filter benefit my hair/skin?

The Qure Shower Filter removes harmful impurities like heavy metals, chlorine, and unwanted minerals from your water, helping to protect your skin and hair from damage caused by hard water.

By filtering out water impurities, the Qure Shower Filter helps reduce dryness, irritation, and brittleness, leaving your skin hydrated and your hair smoother and more manageable.

Does the filter lose effectiveness over time?

Yes, the filter removes up to 99% of chlorine initially, 95% after 4,000 liters, and 90% after 6,000 liters. For best results, we recommend replacing it after 5,000 liters (around 3 months).

We recommend replacing the filter every 3 months for optimal performance.

How do I know when to replace the filter?

A slight drop in water pressure or visible buildup on the filter may indicate it’s time for a replacement. We recommend changing it every 3 months to maintain optimal performance.

How do I install the new filter refill?
  • Turn off the water.
  • Open the filter housing and remove the old cartridge.
  • Unwrap the new cartridge and insert it into the housing.
  • Secure the cover by screwing it back clockwise.
  • Turn on the water and let it run for 2–3 minutes to flush out any initial particles.
Is the Qure Shower Filter certified, tested, and safe to use?

Yes — it’s third-party tested to meet NSF water filtration standards and made with certified BPA-free, non-toxic, and skin-friendly materials. It’s also leak-resistant and built to last.
